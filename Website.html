About

Lifting the words off the page and giving your imagination a voice. In every project, Jacob brings care and thoughtfulness to the roles he plays and his own brand of imaginative charm behind the scenes. With his natural British accent every project from narration to commercials to whatever you need is delivered with sincerity.

Enhance your work with a performance that enthralls, captivates and connects. As a versatile actor and emotive human being, Jacob brings depth to your characters of all shapes and sizes. Trustworthy narrators, innocent heroes, and fantastical villains.

For Jacob, your script is another opportunity to tell a story, whether it be of bold knights, a far flung future, or the friend next door telling you how to chop a carrot. As lover of storytelling Jacob is well versed in its many forms: Animation, video games, fantasy and tabletop RPGs all made more real with his voice behind them.
